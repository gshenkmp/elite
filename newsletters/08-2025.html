Service Highlights

Retirement & Guaranteed Income: We helped clients build robust retirement strategies with 8 Annuities, including Multi-Year Guaranteed Annuities (MYGAs) and Fixed Index Annuities with income riders designed to create a personal pension.

Tax-Advantaged Retirement Planning: We opened 5 IRA accounts funded with powerful annuity solutions to help clients grow their retirement savings with tax benefits.

Life Insurance with Living Benefits: We protected families with 6 IUL policies and 2 Term policies that include living benefits or Long-Term Care (LTC) riders, giving them access to funds in case of a critical or chronic illness.

Specialized Care Solutions: We designed a dedicated Long-Term Care (LTC) solution to protect a couple together, ensuring they have a plan in place for future healthcare needs.

Securing the Next Generation: We helped families give their children a financial head start by setting up 4 Juvenile IUL policies.

"Every family we serve represents a commitment to financial security and peace of mind that will last for generations."
