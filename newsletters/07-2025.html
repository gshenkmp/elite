Service Highlights

Final Expense Insurance: Provided peace of mind for senior families by ensuring end-of-life expenses won't burden loved ones, with policies averaging $15,000 in coverage.

Investment Strategy Highlights

Traditional & Roth IRAs: Helped 5 individuals maximize their retirement savings with tax-advantaged accounts. Our comprehensive analysis determined the optimal contribution strategies based on current income, tax brackets, and retirement timeline projections.

SEP IRAs: Established retirement plans for 2 small business owners, enabling them to contribute up to 25% of their income while providing the same benefits to their employees - a win-win for business growth and team retention.

Fixed Annuity: Secured guaranteed income for a client entering retirement, providing the stability and predictability they desired for their golden years.

Mutual Fund Investment: Strategically positioned a client's portfolio with a diversified mutual fund aligned with their risk tolerance and 10-year investment horizon.

"Our holistic approach ensures every investment decision supports our clients' broader financial goals and life aspirations."
